Chicken and Rice Recipie

Ingredients:

  • 4 Chicken thigh, bone-in, skin-on
  • 2 cups of jasmine white Rice
  • 4 Cups of Chicken Broth
  • 1 Medium onion, Dice
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic, minced
  • 1 Tablespoon of unsalted butter
  • Green Onion, Chopped (Garnish)
  • Seasoning:
    • 2 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp pepper
    • 1 Tablespoon Paprika
    • 1/2 Tablespoon Garlic Powder
    • 1/2 Tablespoon Onion Powder

Directions:

  1. Turn the stove on to Medium high heat. Add Oil/butter and let melt
  2. While Butter is melting, chop up onion and mince the Garlic
  3. We need to season the chicken on both sides. we are going to place the chicken, skin side down on the skillet. cook for abou 6 minutes or until brown. Flip over and cook for another 3 minutes.
  4. Remove the chicken, add another 1 Tbs. of butter to the pan, throw in onions and cook for 2 minutes, until soft. Then add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
  5. Add in rice and toast the rice for like 2 minutes. Then add chicken broth, seasoning, and place chicken back in the pot. Cover. Once boiling, lower heat to a simmer. Cook for like 20 miutes, until liquid is soaked in rice.
  6. Once the chicken is 165°F, Serve. Add onion for garnish
